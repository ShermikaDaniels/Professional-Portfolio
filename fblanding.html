We must remain dilgent when it comes to security! veiw below to learn more about the Phish.

How to Spot a Phish:



  • Email Address: Do you recongize the email address? Never click on an email when it's an email address you don't regonize. Also if the email comes from a public email domain it's a good idea to use caution no legitamte organization will send emails from a public email domain like gmail, yahoo, etc.

  • Links and Attachments: Never click on strange links or download strange attachments

  • Misspellings and Grammer: Always keep a look out for any misspellings or grammer errors. Sometimes domain names will have typos, this is called typosquatting. typosquatting is used to make the domain look as close to the legimate domain as possible.

  • Urgency: if the message makes you feel a sense of urgency, makes you feel the need to act immediatly this is done on purpose to make you act without thinking.

Education is key, learn to spot different phishing attacks and report them!
