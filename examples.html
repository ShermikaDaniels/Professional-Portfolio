Phishing Template Examples



In my current role as a Cyber Security Training Specialist, I am responsible for developing Phish simulation templates and crafting landing pages tailored to the unique needs of our clients. For a glimpse into my work, please take a look below to view some illustrative examples of the templates I have created.



The goal of a phishing attack is to get the user to act without thinking, this is usually done by creating a sense of urgency.
Phishing is the fraudulent practice of sending emails or other messages purporting to be from reputable companies to induce individuals to reveal personal information, such as passwords and credit card numbers.
